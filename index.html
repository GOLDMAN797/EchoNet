Privacy Policy

Echo Net · Apple TV
Effective date: June 2026 Version 1.0 Zero tracking

Echo Net does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything happens entirely on your device — there are no servers, no accounts, and no tracking of any kind.

What Echo Net Is

Echo Net is a VPN client for Apple TV. You add your own server configurations — WireGuard / AmneziaWG, or a proxy such as VLESS or SOCKS5 — and the app routes your traffic through them. Everything runs on your device. We have no accounts, no servers, and no backend of any kind.

Summary

  • All processing happens on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server.
  • We don't have servers. Echo Net has no backend — there is nothing to sync and no account to create.
  • No analytics, no ads, no trackers. The app does not track your activity and contains no third-party data-collection SDKs.
  • Your traffic goes through the server you configured. It is never routed through us, and we never see it.

Data Stored on Your Device

The servers you add — via QR code, paste, or manually — are saved only locally on your Apple TV, using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is accessible only to the app itself.

Your Traffic

While a connection is active, your network traffic goes through the server you configured. The app does not log, inspect, or transmit your traffic to us or any third party — all tunnel processing happens on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • No traffic logs sent to us or any third party
  • No access to contacts, calendar, photos, or the microphone
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No accounts, no servers, no cloud synchronization

Device Access

Echo Net may request the following permissions, all used only on your device:

  • Local Network — to import a server configuration from a nearby device, for example by scanning a QR code or receiving a config from your iPhone on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • VPN Configuration — to create the network tunnel that routes your traffic through the server you configured. This is required for any VPN app to function.

We do not request access to contacts, calendar, photos, the microphone, or any other personal data.

Distribution through the Apple App Store is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third Parties

The app contains no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. The servers you connect to are operated by you or your provider and are governed by their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Echo Net is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data. Since the app does not collect data at all, it poses no privacy risks to children.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times. You can delete any server in the app whenever you like. All data is stored only on your device, so to remove everything simply delete the app.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Significant changes will be communicated through an app update notice.
